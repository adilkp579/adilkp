Introduction

Ishaath Public School was established in 1994 to provide quality modern education to the students of Poonoor and its surroundings and affiliated to CBSE in 2003. We are privileged to extend our gratitude to A.P. Aboobacker Musliyar for his blessings, affection and patronage.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To optimize the innate talents of the students to enable them to become technologically and academically competent global citizens who are proud of our heritage and can serve the nation with integrity.

Our Mission

Through outstanding teaching-learning techniques incorporating the latest technology, IPS will inspire students to achieve their academic and personal potential, become passionate, reflective learners, and lead healthy, active and balanced lives.

Academic Achievements

During 2023-24, 41 students appeared for the CBSE class 10th exam with 100% pass. 26 students scored above 75%, 13 students scored more than 60%. Ms Fathima Shifa P K scored 467 marks (93.4%) and stood first in the school.

This year 47 students will appear in the 10th std board exam. The total number of students from classes 1 to 10 is 1302. In LKG and UKG the total number of kids is 303.

Co-curricular Activities

  • CCA competitions (writing, drawing, speaking, coloring) for all classes
  • Arts fest for classes 3 to 10
  • IAME Artorium competitions at school, district and state levels
  • School parliament election with Head Boy and Head Girl
  • Exhibitions in Maths, Social Science, IT and Work Experience
  • Special Day Celebrations and Debate competitions

Sports & Games

Annual sports day was conducted for all classes from LKG to class 10. Regular practice classes were provided twice a week in football, badminton, kung fu, archery, skating, yoga and various games.

Infrastructure Development

  • Well-stocked, spacious and digitalized library with 7,000+ books
  • Well-equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Maths and Computer Science
  • ICT Interactive Classrooms with smart boards and internet connectivity
  • New website launched: www.ipspoonoor.co.in with new logo
