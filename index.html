Annual Sports Day 2024

Date: December 10, 2024

Time: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Venue: School Ground

Join us for our Annual Sports Day, a celebration of athleticism, teamwork, and sportsmanship. This year's event promises to be more exciting than ever with:

  • Track and field events for all age groups
  • Team sports competitions
  • Special races for parents and teachers
  • Award ceremony with trophies and medals
  • Food stalls and refreshments

All students are encouraged to participate and showcase their athletic talents. Parents and guardians are warmly invited to cheer for our young athletes.

Science Exhibition 2024

Date: December 15, 2024

Time: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Venue: School Auditorium

Our annual Science Exhibition showcases the incredible scientific curiosity and innovation of our students. This year's theme is "Sustainable Solutions for Tomorrow".

Students from grades 6-12 will present their projects covering various scientific disciplines:

  • Renewable energy models
  • Environmental conservation projects
  • Robotics and automation
  • Biotechnology innovations
  • Space and astronomy models

Special judges from local universities and industries will evaluate the projects. Prizes will be awarded for the most innovative and practical solutions.

Parent-Teacher Meeting

Date: December 20, 2024

Time: 2:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Venue: Respective Classrooms

We cordially invite all parents and guardians to our quarterly Parent-Teacher Meeting. This is an important opportunity to:

  • Discuss your child's academic progress
  • Understand their strengths and areas for improvement
  • Collaborate with teachers on learning strategies
  • Address any concerns or questions
  • Learn about upcoming academic activities

Schedule:

  • 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM: Grades 1-5
  • 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM: Grades 6-8
  • 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM: Grades 9-12

Please bring your child's report card and any specific questions you may have for the teachers.
